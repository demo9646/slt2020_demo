----------------------------> Model Architecture <-----------------------




The codes of this research are available here.

CWT-VAWGAN: VAW-GAN system that converts spectrum and CWT-based F0 (with no conditioning on decoder);
C-VAWGAN: Converts the spectrum with VAW-GAN conditioned on LG-based F0 without CWT decomposition, where F0 is converted with LG-based linear transformation;
C-CWT-VAWGAN (Proposed): Converts the spectrum with VAW-GAN conditioned on CWT-based F0, where F0 is converted with VAW-GAN with CWT decomposition.
